About us

MY Pharmacay chain established to introduce a new trend in the industry "the smart pharmacy" that will serve customers through an advanced "state of the art" electronic system. We strive to lead the community pharmacy market to new standards and make pharmacy service more accessible through adding new applications which will create new health services that provide an extraordinary care to customers. We know our role towards our customer's health and wellness, and we act accordingly. We believe that continuous innovation and constant improvement of our systems, operations, and staff will enable us to embody our vision and accomplish our mission.
